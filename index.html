Essential Travel Tips

Make your pilgrimage comfortable and memorable

‚è∞ Best Time to Visit

  • October-March for most temples
  • April-November for Kedarnath
  • Avoid monsoon for mountain regions
  • Shravan month (July-August) for special experience

üéí What to Pack

  • Comfortable cotton clothes
  • Warm clothes for Kedarnath
  • Modest attire for temples
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • First aid kit and medicines
  • ID proofs and permits

üôè Temple Etiquette

  • Remove shoes before entering
  • Dress modestly covering shoulders and legs
  • No leather items in most temples
  • Photography restrictions may apply
  • Maintain silence and respect
  • Follow guide instructions

üè• Health & Safety

  • Carry altitude sickness medicines for Kedarnath
  • Stay hydrated during travel
  • Avoid street food in remote areas
  • Keep emergency contacts handy
  • Travel in groups for safety
  • Inform family about itinerary
